WELCOME

Welcome to Promisedland Baptist Church. We are so glad that you have chosen this place of worship to honour the Almighty LORD'S name with us. If you are new to this church, we graciously thank you for worshiping with us and we warmly welcome you into Promised Land Baptist Church as our beloved brothers and sisters

It is an Alberta-based church with a unique message.
It's an international Evangelical christmatic Christian denomination.
The headquaters is located in 6th Avenue, 206 Cavera Road, Festac Town Lagos.

The church was founded in March 8th 1987 by Pastor Dr.Oluwaseyi Moses.
It's mission and vision are to promote word of faith, win souls for Christ and to atain eternal life.
The organization has since become a global network of churches with over 9 million members in 47 different countries world wide.
There are 12 areas of emphasis as the 12 pillars of our commission, these pillars are;

  • FAITH
  • THE WORD
  • THE SUPERNATURAL
  • PRAISE
  • THE HOLY SPIRIT
  • PROSPERITY
  • VISION
  • PRAYER
  • WISDOM
  • HEALING
  • CONSECRATION
  • SUCCESS
