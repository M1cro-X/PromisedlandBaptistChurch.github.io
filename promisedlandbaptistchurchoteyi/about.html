History

The ministry of Jesus liad the foundation for the Church, Christ's body on earth.
The church is a living organism focused on exalting its Divine Head, Christ Himself.
By leading sinners to be saved the church glorifies God, witnesses changed lives, and reconstructs the whole socities.
Above all else, Saints' Missionary Church is one arm of the larger body of Christ.

Early Foundation

It was the great commission that brought Pastor Dr.Oluwaseyi Moses into the beautiful, yet sparsely-populated valley to establish Missionary churches in the early 1800s. Although these churches eventually regarded the spiritual standards and sacred truth as outmoded, thier legacy had undoubtedly paved the way for a later holiness work. The Holy spirit had clearly directed some men God to this location. The tent was removed and The Church was built.
